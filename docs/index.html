How to Use — Step by Step

1
Select Month & Year

Pick the month and year for which you want to generate the shift roster. The IRM Weekend (3rd Saturday-Sunday) is auto-detected and shown below.

2
Add Holidays

Click "Add TCS Holiday" or "Add Cigna Holiday" to add holiday dates. Click again to add more dates.

💡 Multiple holidays? Click the "Add" button multiple times — each click adds a new date picker. Add as many holidays as needed.
3
Add Planned Leaves

Select the employee name, then pick From date and To date. All dates in between will automatically be marked as Leave.

💡 Multi-day leave? Set From = first day, To = last day. E.g., "From: Mar 5" → "To: Mar 8" = 4 days of leave.
💡 Single day? Set both From and To to the same date.
💡 Multiple people? Click "Add Leave" once per person (or per date range for the same person).
4
Add Comp-Offs

Same as Leaves — pick employee, From date, To date. All dates in range will be marked CompOff.

💡 Example: Deepak worked on a Saturday and gets Monday-Tuesday off as CompOff → Employee: Deepak, From: Monday, To: Tuesday.
5
Add Force Assignments (Optional)

Use this to force a specific person to a specific shift on a specific date. Useful for IRM weekends or critical deployments.

💡 IRM Weekend: Force your senior engineers to A/B shift on the IRM Saturday & Sunday.
💡 Date range: Pick From and To dates — the person will be locked to that shift for all dates in range.
6
Generate & Download

Click the purple button. The roster generates instantly and the colored Excel file auto-downloads. A preview table also appears on the page.
